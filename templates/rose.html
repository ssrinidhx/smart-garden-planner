Rose

Rosa

Category: Flowers

These often fragrant flowers are at home in any garden, including edibles (the fruit, or hip, is edible to humans as well as wildlife). Roses can be climbers/ramblers, upright, or spreading.

Quick Info

Spacing

2 Squares

Soil Type

Rich, loamy soil

Sun

Full Sun

Water

2 in/week

Germination

3-4 weeks

Sprout to Maturity

6-12 months

Season

Perennial

Frost Resistance

Tolerant

Soil pH

Slightly Acidic to Neutral Soil

Planting Considerations

Roses like rich, organic soil and do not like to be crowded. Avoid high wind areas when possible.

Feeding

Fertilize at the beginning of growing season and again after first bloom cycle is done.
