About Us

Sproutify is an innovative online platform designed to help gardening enthusiasts optimize their plant care and gardening schedules. Users can input their plant preferences to receive tailored recommendations for suitable plants and gardening tasks. The website features an interactive garden planner that allows users to plan planting schedules based on seasonal data. With a user-friendly interface, Sproutify aims to make gardening accessible and enjoyable for both beginners and experienced gardeners alike.
